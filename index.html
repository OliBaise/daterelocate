DateRelocate - Mapping Where Single People Are Moving To

Given Pew Research’s findings that 30% of American adults are single, Date Relocate looked at 2023 US Census Data on marital status, sex and migratory status to work out the states and cities that single men and women were moving to.

Key Findings

  • Alabama is the state with the highest number of single female immigrants compared to male. 135 single women moved to Alabama for every 100 single men.
  • Arkansas is the state with the highest number of single male immigrants compared to women. 133 single men moved to Arkansas for every 100 single women.
  • Raleigh, North Carolina is the city with the highest number of single women moving there compared to single men. 187 single women move to Raleigh for every 100 single men.
  • Huntsville-Decatur-Albertsville, Alabama is the metropolitan area that has the highest number of women moving there compared to men. 197 single women move to Huntsville-Decatur-Albertsville for every 100 single men.

Methodology

To find out which states, cities and Designated Market Areas single men and women are moving to, Date Relocate looked at US Census American Community Survey PUMS microdata for 2023.

More specifically, we looked at people who reported in the survey as:

  • Being over the age of 15.
  • Having lived in their current home for less than one year.
  • Being unmarried, divorced, or widowed.

We then split this by:

  • Sex (either male or female)
  • State
  • Designated Market Area

Designated Market Areas were determined by mapping Public Use Microdata Areas onto relevant Designated Market Areas.

I was unable to map the Public Use Microdata areas to the following Designated Market Areas, so I left them out of the study:

  • Lake Charles, Louisiana
  • Alpena, Michigan
  • Great Falls, Montana
  • North Platte, Nebraska
  • Charlotteville, Virginia
